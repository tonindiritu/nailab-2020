    What we do

    African Acceleration Programs

    Our acceleration programs have enabled 400+ startups gain access to mentorship and investors. We aim to help startups that work with us develop sustainable growth. Nailab has over 8 years of developing acceleration programs together with our partners, both in Africa and around the world. They are; UNFPA, Jack Ma Foundation, Good-up, Close The Gap etc For More Information


    Entrepreneurship Training

    In the 8 years of working with entrepreneurs in Africa, we have developed capabilities to equip our founders & business owners with financial, operations, legal and funding skills. We have trained over 500 entrepreneurs and target 100,000 in the next 10 years online and offline.     Africa Netprenuer prize

    The Africa Netpreneur Prize Initiative is Jack Ma’s flagship entrepreneur program in Africa led by the Jack Ma Foundation with Nailab as the Africa implementing partner. This initiative aims to identify and spotlight African entrepreneur heroes and their stories to inspire the continent and beyond. Partner Jack Ma Foundation.

    The Next
    Economy

    This is a program that focuses on upscaling businesses or upskilling young people from disadvantaged backgrounds, between the ages of 18-30, in readiness for the job market and or build sustainable and thriving businesses. The program which is in its 3 year, focuses on core life skills, employability and entrepreneurship training. Partner: Goodup and SOS Kenya

    Leap 2


    LEAP² is an online platform for jumping the digital divide and LEAP to the next level. The innovations close the digital gap through the use of information and communication technology (ICT) for economic development. LEAP² aims to facilitate the digital transition, create (self-) employment and promote socially responsible entrepreneurship. Partner Goodup, Close the Gap
